Curvilinear Probe: Uses a curved transducer array to generate a sector-shaped field of view with a wide view at depth.

Navigation Mode:

Absolute Position

Movement Keys:
←/→: X axis

W/S: Z axis
Rotation Keys:
Q/E: Yaw (Z)
